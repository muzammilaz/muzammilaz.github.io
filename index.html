Biography

Hi, I am an aspiring researcher, I have been affliated with the applied machine learning domain since 2018, spending over 3 years in industry developing data driven solutions for clients, I have developed keen interest in AI research, specifically, in Computer Vision.

Since winter 2021, I have been a Master’s Student in Germany at FAU Erlangen-Nürnberg. Where I have been learning through projects and courses about cognitive neuroscience inspired robust visual representations, techniques for 3D reconstruction, and Prior-based learning for vision. My current research interests are visual representation learning for 2D and 3D prediction and synthesis with priors learned ideally in a self-supervised manner.

Open for PhD / Research Engineer / R&D Engineer opportunities.
